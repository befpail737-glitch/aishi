Solar Inverter & Energy Storage Solutions

Capacitor solutions for renewable energy applications with high ripple current and temperature performance

Overview

Solar inverters and energy storage systems present challenging requirements for capacitors due to high switching frequencies, elevated temperatures, and demanding ripple current specifications. Aishi offers specialized capacitor solutions designed to meet these requirements with enhanced reliability and extended operational life.

Detailed Application Notes

Solar inverters operate with high switching frequencies that result in significant ripple currents in the DC-link capacitors. Aishi's specialized solar-grade electrolytic capacitors feature enhanced ripple current handling capabilities and improved life characteristics under high-temperature conditions.

Key Design Considerations:

  • Thermal management for extended life in outdoor installations
  • Derating for ripple current handling at elevated temperatures
  • Parallel connection techniques for higher ripple current
  • Compliance with grid-tie inverter standards

Our engineering team has developed reference designs for various inverter topologies including string inverters, central inverters, and energy storage systems.
