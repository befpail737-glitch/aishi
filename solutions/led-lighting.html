Core Challenges in LED Lighting Applications

High Ambient Temperature

LED luminaires often operate in high-temperature environments where standard capacitors fail prematurely.

Extended Operational Life

LED lifetimes often exceed 50,000 hours, requiring capacitors that can match this longevity.

High Ripple Current

Switching power supplies generate significant ripple current that can stress capacitors.

Space Constraints

Compact LED packages require miniaturized components without sacrificing performance.
