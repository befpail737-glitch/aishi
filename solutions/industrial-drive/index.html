Industrial Drive Solutions

Capacitor solutions for variable frequency drives and servo systems

Overview

Industrial drives and servo systems operate in harsh environments with high temperatures, vibration, and electrical stress. Aishi offers robust capacitor solutions designed for the demanding requirements of motor drives, including high ripple current capability, vibration resistance, and extended operational life.

Detailed Application Notes

Industrial drives are subjected to harsh conditions including high temperatures, vibration, and electrical transients. Aishi's industrial-grade capacitors are specifically designed to withstand these conditions with enhanced construction and materials optimized for long-term reliability.

Key Design Considerations:

  • Mounting methods to withstand vibration
  • Thermal management in enclosed drives
  • Derating for extended life under stress
  • Compliance with industrial safety standards

Our engineering team provides application-specific recommendations for implementing Aishi capacitors in various industrial drive topologies.
