EV OBC & BMS Solutions

Capacitor solutions for electric vehicle onboard chargers and battery management systems

Overview

Electric vehicle (EV) applications demand capacitors with automotive-grade reliability, high temperature performance, and robust construction to withstand the harsh environment of automotive applications. Aishi offers specialized capacitor solutions for onboard chargers (OBC), battery management systems (BMS), and other EV power electronics.

Detailed Application Notes

EV systems operate in a harsh automotive environment with temperature extremes, vibration, and electrical transients. Aishi's automotive-grade capacitors are specifically designed to meet AEC-Q200 qualification requirements with enhanced reliability for safety-critical applications.

Key Design Considerations:

  • AEC-Q200 qualification and automotive standards compliance
  • Thermal management in confined automotive spaces
  • Vibration resistance and mechanical robustness
  • Long-term reliability for vehicle lifecycle
