Overview

LED lighting applications require capacitors that can withstand high temperatures and provide long operational life. Aishi offers specialized electrolytic capacitors designed specifically for LED driver circuits, ensuring reliable performance and extended product life.

Aishi capacitors operate reliably at +105°C, crucial for LED applications in enclosed fixtures with high ambient temperatures.

Detailed Application Notes

LED driver designs often operate at high temperatures due to proximity to LED heat sources and limited space for heat dissipation. Aishi capacitors are engineered with enhanced temperature performance to ensure reliable operation in these demanding conditions.

Key Design Considerations:

  • Operating temperature derating for extended life
  • Ripple current management in high-frequency switching applications
  • ESR impact on efficiency and thermal performance
  • Mounting considerations for thermal management

Our engineering team has developed several reference designs optimized for different LED applications, from residential lighting to high-power industrial fixtures.
