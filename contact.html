Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical lead time for Aishi capacitors?

For in-stock items, we can ship within 24-48 hours. For custom or out-of-stock items, lead time is typically 2-4 weeks depending on the specific product and quantity.

Do you provide technical support for Aishi capacitors?

Yes, our certified Field Application Engineers (FAEs) provide comprehensive technical support including application guidance, circuit design, and troubleshooting.

How can I request samples?

You can request samples through our website form, by contacting your local sales office, or by emailing samples@elec-distributor.com.

Are Aishi capacitors RoHS compliant?

Yes, all Aishi capacitors distributed by LiTong Group are RoHS compliant and meet international environmental standards.
