About LiTong Group

Your trusted authorized Aishi distributor

Company Profile

LiTong Group has been a leading distributor of electronic components for over a decade, with a specialized focus on passive components and power electronics. As an officially authorized distributor of Aishi (Hua Wei) products, we provide comprehensive support for customers worldwide.

Our commitment to quality, service, and technical expertise has established us as a preferred partner for engineers and procurement professionals across various industries including consumer electronics, industrial automation, renewable energy, and automotive.
