Aishi Radial Lead Electrolytic Capacitors

Aishi radial lead aluminum electrolytic capacitors are designed for general purpose applications requiring reliable performance and long operational life. These capacitors provide stable capacitance values with excellent temperature characteristics and low equivalent series resistance (ESR).

As an official Aishi distributor, we offer the complete range of radial lead capacitors with technical support and competitive pricing. Our inventory includes multiple series such as CD11, CD110, and CD293 series with various capacitance and voltage ratings.

Recommended Applications

For LED lighting applications, Aishi's radial lead capacitors with 105°C rating are recommended due to their excellent temperature characteristics and extended operational life. The CD110 series is particularly suitable for applications requiring higher reliability and longer operational lifetime.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine the required capacitance for my power supply?

Capacitance requirements depend on the ripple current, desired voltage regulation, and load conditions. Our technical team can provide detailed calculations based on your specific application parameters.

What's the typical lifetime of Aishi radial lead capacitors?

Aishi radial lead capacitors are rated for 2000 to 4000 hours at rated temperature depending on the series. The actual lifetime in application depends on operating conditions such as applied voltage and ambient temperature.

Can Aishi capacitors be used as replacements for other brands?

Yes, Aishi capacitors can often replace components from other manufacturers. We provide cross-reference guides for replacing Japanese brands like Rubycon and Chemi-con. Contact our technical support for specific recommendations.
