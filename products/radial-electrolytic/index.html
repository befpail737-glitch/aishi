Radial Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors

About Aishi Radial Electrolytic Capacitors

Aishi radial aluminum electrolytic capacitors are designed with lead wires for through-hole mounting. These capacitors offer excellent performance with a wide range of capacitance and voltage ratings, making them suitable for various applications including power supplies, consumer electronics, and industrial controls.

Key Parameters

  • Voltage Range: 6.3V to 500V
  • Capacitance Range: 0.1µF to 220,000µF
  • Temperature Range: -40°C to +105°C
  • Capacitance Tolerance: ±20% (at +20°C, 120Hz)
  • ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance): Low ESR options available

Typical Applications

Power Supplies

Input/output filtering in switch-mode power supplies

Consumer Electronics

TVs, audio equipment, and home appliances

Industrial Control

Motors, PLCs, and control systems

Lighting Systems

LED drivers and ballasts

FAE Application Notes

Our field application engineers recommend radial electrolytic capacitors for applications where through-hole mounting is preferred and moderate to high capacitance values are required. For high-frequency applications with significant ripple current, consider our solid polymer series for lower ESR.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right capacitance value?

The required capacitance depends on your application's ripple current, allowed voltage ripple, and hold-up time requirements. For power supply filtering, higher capacitance reduces output ripple voltage.

What's the difference between radial and snap-in capacitors?

Radial capacitors have both leads on the same side (typically bottom) and are mounted through-hole. Snap-in capacitors have screw terminals and are mounted to the PCB or chassis with nuts.

How do temperature ratings affect performance?

Capacitors should be used within their specified temperature range. Higher temperature ratings generally allow for longer life and better performance in harsh environments.
