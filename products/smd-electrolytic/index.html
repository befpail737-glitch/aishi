About Aishi SMD Electrolytic Capacitors

Aishi SMD aluminum electrolytic capacitors are designed for surface mount applications, providing reliable performance in compact electronic assemblies. These capacitors offer excellent volumetric efficiency and are suitable for automated assembly processes.

Key Parameters

  • Voltage Range: 6.3V to 450V
  • Capacitance Range: 1.0µF to 3300µF
  • Temperature Range: -40°C to +105°C
  • Package Sizes: Diameters from 4mm to 16mm
  • Mounting: Surface mount, compatible with reflow soldering

Selection Guide

Choosing the right SMD electrolytic capacitor involves considering package size constraints, electrical requirements, and environmental conditions. For applications with high ripple current, consider lower ESR options. Read our comprehensive selection guide or contact our FAE team for recommendations.

FAE Application Notes

Our field application engineers recommend SMD electrolytic capacitors for applications where board space is at a premium and through-hole mounting is not preferred. Pay attention to reflow profile compatibility during assembly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can SMD electrolytic capacitors handle high ripple current?

Yes, Aishi SMD electrolytic capacitors are designed to handle significant ripple current. However, for very high ripple current applications, consider using multiple capacitors in parallel or selecting a higher-rated part.

What are the advantages of SMD vs. radial capacitors?

SMD capacitors save board space, allow for automated assembly, and reduce the need for through-holes. However, they typically have lower capacitance and voltage ratings than radial types.

How do I ensure proper reflow soldering?

Follow Aishi's recommended reflow profile, which typically has a peak temperature of 235°C for less than 30 seconds. Avoid excessive heating which can damage the capacitor.
