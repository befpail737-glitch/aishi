New High-Performance Capacitor Series Launched

October 15, 2023 Company News

Aishi, a leading global manufacturer of electronic components, has announced the launch of its new series of high-temperature, long-life electrolytic capacitors with significantly improved performance characteristics for demanding applications.

Enhanced Specifications

The new series features several key improvements over previous generations:

  • Extended operational life up to 15,000 hours at +105Â°C
  • Improved ripple current capability with up to 30% higher ratings
  • Reduced equivalent series resistance (ESR) for better efficiency
  • Enhanced temperature characteristics across the full operating range

Target Applications

These high-performance capacitors are specifically designed for applications requiring extended operational life and high reliability, including:

  • Renewable energy systems (solar inverters, wind power)
  • Industrial automation and motor drives
  • Telecommunications infrastructure
  • Automotive power electronics

Global Availability

The new series is now available through LiTong Group's global distribution network. Initial inventory is available for immediate shipment, with expanded stock levels planned for the coming months.

"We're excited to bring these enhanced performance capacitors to market," said a spokesperson for Aishi. "This new series represents our commitment to continuously improving our products to meet the evolving needs of our customers in demanding applications."

For more information about the new series or to request samples, contact our technical support team through the website or your local sales representative.
