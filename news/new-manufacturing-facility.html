Aishi Announces New Advanced Manufacturing Facility

Home > News > Company News

Company News

Published: November 20, 2023

By Aishi PR Team

Strategic Expansion to Meet Growing Demand for High-Performance Capacitors

Aishi Group has officially broken ground on a new 100,000 square foot manufacturing facility dedicated to the production of solid polymer and high-performance electrolytic capacitors. The facility, located in Jiangsu Province, represents a significant investment in advanced manufacturing capabilities and increased production capacity.

Advanced Manufacturing Capabilities

The new facility features state-of-the-art automation and precision control systems designed to ensure consistent quality and high reliability in capacitor production. The plant incorporates Industry 4.0 principles with real-time monitoring and data analytics to optimize production efficiency and quality.

Key features of the new facility include:

  • ISO 14644 Class 7 cleanroom environments for critical manufacturing processes
  • Automated material handling systems to minimize contamination
  • Advanced process control systems with real-time quality monitoring
  • Integrated testing and validation capabilities
  • Environmentally sustainable processes with recycling systems

Increased Capacity for Solid Polymer Capacitors

The facility will significantly increase production capacity for Aishi's solid polymer capacitor series, which have seen growing demand in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics applications. The solid polymer capacitors produced at this facility will feature ultra-low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance), high ripple current capability, and excellent frequency characteristics.

According to Aishi's Director of Manufacturing, Dr. Wei Zhang, "The new facility positions Aishi to meet the rapidly growing demand for high-performance capacitors while maintaining the exceptional quality our customers expect. Our investment in advanced manufacturing technology ensures we can deliver reliable components that meet the most demanding application requirements."

Focus on High-Performance Electrolytic Capacitors

In addition to solid polymer capacitors, the facility will produce high-performance aluminum electrolytic capacitors including long-life series for LED lighting and automotive applications, and low-impedance series for switching power supplies. These capacitors feature extended temperature ranges, high ripple current capability, and long operational life.

The new facility will also house R&D capabilities, allowing for rapid prototyping and development of next-generation capacitor technologies. This integration of development and production capabilities is expected to accelerate product innovation and time-to-market for new capacitor solutions.

Global Supply Chain Impact

The new manufacturing facility will strengthen Aishi's global supply chain and improve delivery times for customers worldwide. The facility is expected to achieve full production capacity by the end of 2024, with an annual production capacity of over 500 million units.

As an authorized Aishi distributor, LiTong Group will benefit from this increased capacity and improved supply chain reliability, enabling us to better serve our customers with shorter lead times and enhanced availability of Aishi capacitors.

Environmental and Quality Commitments

The facility incorporates environmentally sustainable manufacturing processes and has achieved ISO 14001 environmental management certification during construction. All processes are designed to minimize environmental impact while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Quality control systems include 100% electrical testing of products, statistical process control, and comprehensive reliability testing. The facility implements Aishi's Total Quality Management system to ensure consistent high quality across all products.

The new manufacturing facility represents Aishi's commitment to meeting growing global demand for high-performance capacitors and supporting the continued advancement of electronic systems across all industries. This investment ensures Aishi's position as a leading supplier of advanced capacitor solutions.
