About LiTong Group

Home > About Us

Official Authorized Aishi Distributor

LiTong Group is an official authorized一级 (first-level) agent of Aishi Group, one of the world's leading manufacturers of aluminum electrolytic capacitors. Since our establishment, we have been committed to providing customers worldwide with authentic Aishi products, comprehensive technical support, and competitive pricing.

As an Aishi distributor, we offer the complete range of Aishi capacitors including radial lead, SMD, snap-in, solid polymer, and film capacitors. Our partnership with Aishi enables us to provide customers with reliable, high-performance capacitors for applications across multiple industries.
